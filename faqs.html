F.A.Qs

Frequently asked question

What types of books do you sell?
We offer a wide variety of books, including Action, Romance, Comic Books, Fantasy, Horror, Literary Fiction, Historical titles, and educational topics like Astronomy and Geography. We also have a collection of free books for our readers.

Do you offer free books or collections?
Yes! Our Free Collections section includes a curated list of zero-cost books for readers who love discovering new titles without spending a dime.

Can I buy books online and get them delivered?
Absolutely. You can place your order directly through our website, and we’ll deliver your books to your doorstep. Delivery times depend on your location.

Are eBooks or audiobooks available for these categories?
Yes, many of our genres — including Romance, Fantasy, Horror, and Astronomy — are available as eBooks and audiobooks for instant download.

How can I find books by genre?
You can browse by category such as Action, Comic Books, Sport, Geography, and more using our website’s navigation or search filters.

Do you offer student-friendly or educational books?
We do! Subjects like Astronomy, Geography, and History are covered in our academic and reference sections, perfect for students and curious minds.

How do I get help or request a specific book?
If you’re looking for a specific title or need assistance, you can reach out via our Contact page. We’re happy to help you find what you’re looking for or place a special order.


Other Question

General questions




What are your store hours and contact details?
Our bookstore is open Monday to Saturday from 9 AM to 8 PM. For inquiries, you can reach us via email, phone, or through the contact form on our website.

Do I need to create an account to place an order?
No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save your favorites, and enjoy a faster checkout experience.
